description: CariRuang is a platform that helps you find workspaces that suit your needs, whether you're freelancing, running a business, or working remotely, making work more convenient and productive for Indonesians. After the pandemic, many people are working remotely, and they need a place to work. CariRuang helps them find a workspace that suits their needs. It also helps workspace owners to promote their workspace and attract more customers.
